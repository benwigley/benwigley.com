Electrical Components

Blue Sea Systems Blade Fuse Block - 12 circuit - With cover and negative block You will connect the red wires of all your 12v appliances to individual red terminals on this fuse block. You can then insert the appropriate amp blade fuse for your appliance to prevent it causing a fire if the appliance has an electrical issue. This fuse block also comes with a block to connect all your common (black/negative wires). These don't need to be connected in any special order or arrangement.
Bayite Digital DC Meter with 100A Current Shunt You'll want to know a few things about your system to ensure you converse your power and don't drain your batteries below their minimum level. This is a measuring system that you wire into your electrical board. It will tell you things like how full your battery it, and how much power you are drawing. It comes with easy to follow wiring instructions.
Zip Tie Adhesive-Backed Mounts - 100 Pack When wiring up your electrical board you'll want your wires organised to prevent confusion. Zip ties are a great way to do this. These zip tie mounts are really easy to use, simply peal off the plastic backing and stick them down to your electrical board.
Cable Ties - 100 Pack - Black To go along with your cable tie mounts.
140 Amp Dual Battery Smart Isolator If you want to charge your house batteries while you drive then you'll want one of these. It's free elctricity that would otherwise go to waste, so I recommend doign this.
SolarEpic Battery Temperature Sensor and Monitoring adapter for Tracer MPPT A Battery Temperature Sensor will prevent your batteries overheating while charging from your solar panels. If the solar controller detects a high temperature it wil automatically stop charging.
ACOPOWER 40A MPPT Solar Charge Controller + Remote Meter A solar controller of 40 amps is pretty big. Google around for how many amps you will need for the amount of panels you have. Either way, I'd still recommend this controller. Just click on the link and select a smaller amp size if you wish. You can choose between 10A, 20A, 30A, and 40A. Buying the remote meter that comes in this package is definitely worth it as it is much easier to read then the LCD screen on the controller itself.
4-Post Power Distribution Blocks – Pair of 5/16” Black & 3/8” Red These are heavy duty terminals that allow you to connect large 12v components. Things like your Inverter, VSR (for charging from your alternator), and your solar controller. These bus blocks aren't stricktly necessary, you could connect things directly to your batteries, but your battery terminals would end up pretty untidy.
